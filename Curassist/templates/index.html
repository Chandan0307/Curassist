How curassist is making health data management better?

  • hassle free life

    Curassist is a software that will store all the medical history in one place of a patient, apart from this it provides the facility to give specific access to data to doctors, pharmacies, and pathologists.

  • dectection of diseases

    Severe diseases are detected at the earliest. As the patient uploads the test report “curassist” will predict the future diseases that the person might suffer from through machine learning models.

  • Secure Your Data

    No compromise with the confidentiality of the medical data of a patient from now as “curassist” will provide end-to-end protection to the stored data of patients using blockchain technology.
